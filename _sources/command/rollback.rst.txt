.rollback
-----------

``.rollback`` aborts and rolls back the current transaction.

JDBC Call
~~~~~~~~~

It corresponds to JDBC
`Connection <https://docs.oracle.com/javase/8/docs/api/java/sql/Connection.html>`__
class's
`rollback() <https://docs.oracle.com/javase/8/docs/api/java/sql/Connection.html#rollback-->`__
function.
